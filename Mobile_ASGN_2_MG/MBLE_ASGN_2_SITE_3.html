The Combine

The Combine, an alien empire that conquered the Earth after the Black Mesa Incident. They rule by proxy through one, Wallace Breen. The True rulers are the Combine Advisors, which are part of the Combine Species, The Combine force is split into 6 distinct catagories. The Civil Protection Officers, unaltered humans that work for the Combine. Combine Soliders, altered Humans, these are the standard infantry of the Combine. Noke Prospekt Units, Combine Soldiers in charge of the Nova Prospket Prison. The Overwach Elite, even more altered Elite Soliders. Synths, robots, altered alien animals, and vehicles. Civil workers, includes Combine Workers and Stalkers. The Combine keeps a iron fist over the planet.

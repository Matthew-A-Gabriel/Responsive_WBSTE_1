The Rebels

The Resistance, a decentralized rebel movement opposed to the Combine. Their most prominent Members include Eli Vance, the leader of the Resistance, Issac Kliner and Arne Magnusson, both Scientists who worked in Black Mesa, Barney Calhoun and Odessa Cubbage, Military leaders in the Resistance and Alyx Vance, Ali's daughter. They got their equipment by either picking them up from fallen Combine or smuggling it. They helped citizens escape Combine controlled cities through a underground rail road. they get their info by infiltrating Combine Civil Protection, a low ranking part of the Combine Force.
